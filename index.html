At the end of the day, we're the ship builders of the software world. We love to work on ships of all sizes and shapes and see them set sail as soon as possible. A lot of those ships are still sailing today with minor fixes here and there to keep'em going. That's how we like to do.

How much is this gonna cost me?

Our rates are competitive and most of the time only 2 of our developers are on a project at a time. We charge 2-weeks of developer time up-front before the work is started. We keep to a payment schedule that the next 2 weeks of work are already paid for. It's just easier. We're not bill-collectors, we're software developers. We don't negotiate this part, some have tried and we just don't deviate from this part of our process.

Do we do work on projects for equity?

Our view on equity is extremely strict. We've come close on one project. Our only requirement to have that conversation is we need to have already worked on your platform for 6-weeks.
